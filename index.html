A note

Over the last decade of working across Europe, I learned where I do my best work: the "0 to 1" — the quiet, uncertain space of building something real out of nothing at all.

I didn't set out with a grand plan. I joined small teams and made myself useful: I built Infento's web shop from nothing to €2M, helped take the Swiss brand VU from zero to the Olympic stage, and grew the fintech Bueno from a blank page to €300K+ ARR across 28 countries.

Along the way I noticed the part I love most isn't the launch — it's the teaching. Sharing what a decade of building taught me, and watching someone start to believe in themselves, is the most fulfilling work I know.

If there's value we could create together, I'd love to meet. I'm always happy to know good people building from nothing.
